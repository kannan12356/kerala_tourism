Top Destinations

Alappuzha, often called the "Venice of the East," is a picturesque town in Kerala, India. Known for its serene backwaters, houseboats, and beautiful beaches, it is a hub for tourism. Alappuzha is also famous for its annual Nehru Trophy Boat Race, a vibrant cultural event. The town's lush paddy fields and charming canals make it a popular destination for nature lovers.

Idukki, a beautiful district in Kerala, is known for its lush green hills, sprawling tea plantations, and scenic landscapes. It houses the Idukki Arch Dam, one of the highest in Asia. Idukki is rich in wildlife, with the Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary being a major attraction. Ernakulam, often referred to as the commercial capital of Kerala, is a bustling city that forms part of Kochi. It is known for its vibrant business hubs, historical landmarks like the Hill Palace, and modern infrastructure. Ernakulam is also home to the famous Marine Drive promenade, offering stunning views of the backwaters. The city blends tradition and modernity, making it a key center for trade, tourism, and cultural experiences in Kerala.

Wayanad, a scenic district in Kerala, is renowned for its lush greenery, rolling hills, and rich wildlife. Known for its coffee, tea, and spice plantations, Wayanad offers stunning landscapes dotted with waterfalls, caves, and lakes. Key attractions include Edakkal Caves, with ancient rock carvings, and Wayanad Wildlife Sanctuary, home to elephants, tigers, and other wildlife. Its cool climate and serene atmosphere make Wayanad a popular destination for trekking and nature lovers.

Kerala, known as "God's Own Country," is a beautiful state in southern India, famed for its stunning landscapes, backwaters, and rich cultural heritage. It offers a diverse mix of beaches, hill stations, and wildlife sanctuaries. Kerala is also known for its unique traditions like Kathakali, Ayurvedic treatments, and vibrant festivals such as Onam. With its high literacy rate and focus on healthcare, Kerala stands out for its social progress and is a top destination for both tourism and quality of life.
